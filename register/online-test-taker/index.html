Register to Take your Tests on Askcamel!

I don't have a Registration Code?

Only users who have been given their unique Registration Code by a Askcamel Administrator can register to take their tests.

You cannot register to take your Test without your unique Registration code.

Please ask the person giving you the Test for your unique Registration code.

You can copy the link below and email it to your Instructor to show them how to create a registration code for you.
Your unique Registration code can only be created from their account to give you access to your test.

https://www. Askcamel.com/online-testing/manual/#addmembersintro

Note:
Do not register an Instructor account to take tests. Instructor accounts do not have any tests included, they allow administrators to first Create and then give Tests online, you must register a Test taker account with your unique Registration code to access and take your Test/s on Askcamel.
